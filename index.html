Om migAbout me

Udvikler, ingeniør og kreativ problemløser med fokus på software, spil, AI, visuelle systemer og idéer der kan bygges videre på.Developer, engineer and creative problem-solver focused on software, games, AI, visual systems and ideas that can be expanded into real products.

Jeg hedder Mikael Wendt. Jeg er civilingeniør og softwareudvikler med fokus på C#, Unity, backend-udvikling, AI-pipelines, tekniske værktøjer og komplekse systemer. Jeg arbejder bedst, når der er et svært problem, mange bevægelige dele og behov for både struktur, analyse og praktisk implementering.

Min tekniske baggrund kommer fra computational engineering, strukturel analyse, matematik, machine learning og softwareudvikling. Den bruger jeg i dag til at bygge systemer, der skal kunne mere end bare fungere i en prototype: de skal kunne udvides, debugges, forstås og udvikles videre uden at falde fra hinanden.

Jeg har arbejdet med backend-systemer, spilarkitektur, NPC-adfærd, save/load, netværk, editor tools, dataflow, procedural generation og AI-baserede systemer. Jeg er særligt interesseret i software, hvor teknik, simulation, design og menneskelig adfærd mødes.

Ved siden af udvikling laver jeg også musik, billeder, visuelle koncepter og videoredigering. Det kreative arbejde hænger tæt sammen med mit tekniske arbejde: jeg kan lide at tage en idé, give den form, teste den, justere den og bygge den op til noget konkret.

My name is Mikael Wendt. I am a civil engineer and software developer focused on C#, Unity, backend development, AI pipelines, technical tools and complex systems. I work best when a problem has many moving parts and requires structure, analysis and practical implementation.

My technical background comes from computational engineering, structural analysis, mathematics, machine learning and software development. I use that background to build systems that need to do more than work as prototypes: they need to be extensible, debuggable, understandable and able to grow without collapsing under their own complexity.

I have worked with backend systems, game architecture, NPC behavior, save/load systems, networking, editor tools, data flow, procedural generation and AI-based systems. I am especially interested in software where technology, simulation, design and human behavior meet.

Outside pure development, I also make music, images, visual concepts and video editing. The creative work connects directly to my technical work: I like taking an idea, giving it form, testing it, adjusting it and building it into something concrete.
